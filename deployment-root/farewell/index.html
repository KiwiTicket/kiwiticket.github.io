Thank You

After 5 incredible years, KiwiTicket is saying goodbye

To all our amazing event producers who trusted us with their dreams and to every ticket buyer who discovered something special through our platform, thank you.

For five years, we've been privileged to be part of New Zealand's vibrant events scene. From intimate gigs to outdoor concerts, comedy festivals to high school productions, community fundraisers to cultural celebrations, you made KiwiTicket more than just a ticketing platform, you made it a community.

While this chapter is closing, the memories, connections, and experiences you've all created will live on. Thank you for letting us be part of your journey.

Ngā mihi and farewell,
The KiwiTicket Team
